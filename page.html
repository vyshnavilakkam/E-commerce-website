Indoor Plants

Low-Light Plants

  • Snake Plant (Sansevieria): Perfect for beginners; thrives on neglect.
  • ZZ Plant (Zamioculcas zamiifolia): Hardy and resilient, great for any room.
  • Pothos (Epipremnum aureum): Easy to grow and forgiving, ideal for low light.

Air-Purifying Plants

  • Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum): Elegant blooms and air-purifying qualities.
  • Boston Fern (Nephrolepis exaltata): Adds lush greenery and cleans the air.
  • Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum): Known for its air-purifying abilities and playful spiderettes.
